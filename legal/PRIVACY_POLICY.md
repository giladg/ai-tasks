# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 28, 2026**

## Introduction

AI Task Manager ("we," "our," or "us") operates ai-tasks.app (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Google Account Information**: When you sign in with Google, we collect:
  - Email address
  - Name
  - Profile picture URL
  - Google user ID

### Information We Access (With Your Permission)

When you authorize access, we access:

- **Gmail Data**: We read your Gmail messages to extract task-related information
  - Subject lines
  - Email body content
  - Sender information
  - Dates and times
  - We do NOT access: Attachments, spam, trash, or deleted emails

- **Google Calendar Data**: We read your calendar events to extract tasks
  - Event titles
  - Event descriptions
  - Event dates and times
  - Event locations
  - We do NOT modify or delete any calendar events

### Data We Generate

- **Tasks**: Information extracted from your emails and calendar
  - Task titles
  - Task descriptions
  - Due dates
  - Priority levels
  - Completion status

- **Usage Data**:
  - Last sync time
  - Account creation date
  - Login history

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Service**: Extract tasks from your Gmail and Calendar using AI
2. **Authenticate You**: Verify your identity when you log in
3. **Sync Your Data**: Automatically update tasks based on new emails and events
4. **Improve the Service**: Analyze usage patterns (in aggregate, anonymized form)
5. **Communicate**: Send service-related notifications (if enabled)

## How We Store Your Information

### Security Measures

- **Encryption**: OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using Fernet encryption
- **Secure Transport**: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS
- **Access Control**: Database access is restricted and authenticated
- **Hashing**: Passwords are not stored (we use OAuth only)

### Data Storage

- **OAuth Tokens**: Encrypted and stored in our database
- **Tasks**: Stored in our database with user association
- **Emails/Calendar Events**: NOT stored - only processed transiently for task extraction

### Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers located in the United States.

## Third-Party Services

### Google APIs

We use Google OAuth 2.0 and Google APIs to access your Gmail and Calendar data. Your use of these services is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### AI Processing

We use Google's Gemini AI to process your email and calendar content for task extraction. This processing:
- Happens in real-time during sync
- Does not permanently store your email/calendar content in Gemini systems
- Is subject to Google's AI/ML terms of service

### Infrastructure

We use cloud hosting services for hosting. They have access to server infrastructure but not to decrypted user data.

## Data Retention

- **OAuth Tokens**: Retained while your account is active
- **Tasks**: Retained while your account is active
- **Email/Calendar Content**: NOT retained - processed transiently only
- **Account Data**: Retained until account deletion

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You can:
- **View Your Data**: Access all your tasks through the dashboard
- **Edit Tasks**: Modify extracted tasks
- **Revoke Access**: Disconnect Gmail/Calendar access at any time
- **Delete Account**: Request complete account deletion

### Revoking Access

To revoke our access to Gmail/Calendar:
1. Visit Google Account Permissions: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
2. Find "AI Task Manager"
3. Click "Remove Access"

### Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Contact us at giladgolan3@gmail.com
2. Or use the account deletion feature in your dashboard (if implemented)

We will delete all your data within 30 days, except:
- Anonymized usage statistics
- Data required for legal compliance
